Welcome

A House That Has
Kept Its Silence

Behind an unmarked gate in Lastra a Signa, a seventeenth-century farmhouse looks out over its own vineyard and olive grove. Stone stairways, weathered shutters, and a wrought-iron well mark a place that was never built to impress — only to last.

Every arrival here is private. There is no shared driveway, no neighbouring wing, no schedule but your own — only the estate, the hills, and whoever you choose to bring with you.
